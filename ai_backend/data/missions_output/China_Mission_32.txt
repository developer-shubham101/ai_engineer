Mission Name: China_Mission_32. Country: China. Date: 1973-02-19. Outcome:
Passed. Type: Lunar sample return. Description: Launched on 1973-02-19,
China_Mission_32 from China served as a Lunar sample return. Primary goals were
to map surface composition, test landing and navigation systems, characterize
the local environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. It achieved its
objectives and provided valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge
and subsequent missions. The mission added to the global understanding of
planetary formation, surface processes, and potential resources.
