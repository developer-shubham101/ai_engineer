Mission Name: Perseverance. Country: USA (NASA). Date: 2020-07-30. Outcome:
Passed. Type: Unmanned Mars rover + sample caching. Description: USA (NASA)'s
Perseverance, launched 2020-07-30, performed a Unmanned Mars rover + sample
caching mission. Primary goals were to map surface composition, test landing and
navigation systems, characterize the local environment, and return telemetry to
ground stations. It achieved its objectives and provided valuable data that
contributed to scientific knowledge and subsequent missions. International
collaborations were often leveraged for instrument contributions and data
analysis.
