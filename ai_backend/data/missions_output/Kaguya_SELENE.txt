Mission Name: Kaguya_SELENE. Country: Japan. Date: 2007-09-14. Outcome: Passed.
Type: Unmanned lunar orbiter. Description: Japan's Kaguya_SELENE, launched
2007-09-14, performed a Unmanned lunar orbiter mission. Primary goals were to
map surface composition, test landing and navigation systems, characterize the
local environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. It achieved its
objectives and provided valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge
and subsequent missions. The mission added to the global understanding of
planetary formation, surface processes, and potential resources.
