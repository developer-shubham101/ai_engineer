Mission Name: Beresheet. Country: Israel. Date: 2019-02-21. Outcome: Failed.
Type: Unmanned lunar lander (crash). Description: Beresheet was a Unmanned lunar
lander (crash) launched by Israel on 2019-02-21. Primary goals were to map
surface composition, test landing and navigation systems, characterize the local
environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. The mission encountered
critical failures and did not meet its primary objectives, but lessons learned
informed later programs. The mission added to the global understanding of
planetary formation, surface processes, and potential resources.
