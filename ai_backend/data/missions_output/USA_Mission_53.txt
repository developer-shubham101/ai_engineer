Mission Name: USA_Mission_53. Country: USA. Date: 1986-11-18. Outcome: Passed.
Type: Lunar sample return. Description: USA's USA_Mission_53, launched
1986-11-18, performed a Lunar sample return mission. Primary goals were to map
surface composition, test landing and navigation systems, characterize the local
environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. It achieved its objectives
and provided valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge and
subsequent missions. The mission added to the global understanding of planetary
formation, surface processes, and potential resources.
