Mission Name: ESA_Mission_42. Country: ESA. Date: 1979-04-22. Outcome: Passed.
Type: Planetary flyby. Description: ESA_Mission_42 was a Planetary flyby
launched by ESA on 1979-04-22. Primary goals were to map surface composition,
test landing and navigation systems, characterize the local environment, and
return telemetry to ground stations. It achieved its objectives and provided
valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge and subsequent missions.
The mission added to the global understanding of planetary formation, surface
processes, and potential resources.
