Mission Name: South_Mission_49. Country: South Korea. Date: 1983-06-08. Outcome:
Partial Success. Type: Crewed lunar mission. Description: South Korea's
South_Mission_49, launched 1983-06-08, performed a Crewed lunar mission mission.
Primary goals were to map surface composition, test landing and navigation
systems, characterize the local environment, and return telemetry to ground
stations. Some objectives were met while others were not; the partial success
still yielded scientific returns and engineering lessons. Onboard instruments
typically included imagers, spectrometers, magnetometers, and sometimes landers
