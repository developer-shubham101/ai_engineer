Mission Name: Aditya-L1. Country: India. Date: 2023-09-02. Outcome: Passed.
Type: Unmanned solar observatory (L1). Description: India's Aditya-L1, launched
2023-09-02, performed a Unmanned solar observatory (L1) mission. Primary goals
were to map surface composition, test landing and navigation systems,
characterize the local environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. It
achieved its objectives and provided valuable data that contributed to
scientific knowledge and subsequent missions. The mission added to the global
understanding of planetary formation, surface processes, and potential
resources.
