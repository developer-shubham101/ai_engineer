Mission Name: Venera_7. Country: USSR. Date: 1970-12-15. Outcome: Passed. Type:
Unmanned Venus lander. Description: USSR's Venera_7, launched 1970-12-15,
performed a Unmanned Venus lander mission. Primary goals were to map surface
composition, test landing and navigation systems, characterize the local
environment, and return telemetry to ground stations. It achieved its objectives
and provided valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge and
subsequent missions. International collaborations were often leveraged for
instrument contributions and data analysis.
