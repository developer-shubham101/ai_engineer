Mission Name: Hayabusa2. Country: Japan. Date: 2014-12-03. Outcome: Passed.
Type: Unmanned asteroid sample return. Description: Launched on 2014-12-03,
Hayabusa2 from Japan served as a Unmanned asteroid sample return. Engineers used
this flight to validate hardware, improve communications, and gather operational
experience for follow-up missions. It achieved its objectives and provided
valuable data that contributed to scientific knowledge and subsequent missions.
Onboard instruments typically included imagers, spectrometers, magnetometers,
and sometimes landers or rovers for in-situ study.
