## About Us: Agni Holdings Inc.

Welcome to Agni Holdings Inc., a global conglomerate built on a legacy of innovation, strategic foresight, and a relentless pursuit of excellence since 1956. Inspired by the Sanskrit word "Agni," symbolizing fire, transformation, and knowledge, our journey began with a vision to ignite progress across vital industries. Today, Agni Holdings stands as a multi-billion dollar enterprise, driven by a diverse portfolio of companies that guide, create, analyze, and deliver solutions crucial for the 21st century.

With a presence across six major global hubs—Mumbai, London, New York, Singapore, Frankfurt, Tokyo, and Bengaluru—we leverage our worldwide reach to foster groundbreaking advancements in pharmaceuticals, information technology, data analytics, logistics, and digital experiences. Our strength lies in our interconnected ecosystem of specialized subsidiaries, each a leader in its field, united by the Agni ethos of integrity, innovation, and client-centricity. We believe in nurturing a culture where every individual is empowered to contribute to a brighter, more connected, and healthier future.

### Our Vision

To be the transformative force that empowers industries and enriches lives globally, through pioneering innovation, unwavering quality, and sustainable solutions.

### Our Mission

To guide our clients through complex challenges, engineer cutting-edge products, and leverage data-driven insights to create unparalleled value, while fostering a culture of continuous learning and ethical practice across our diverse global operations.

### What We Do

Agni Holdings Inc. operates through a synergistic network of specialized companies, each focusing on critical sectors:

#### Saarthi Infotech Pvt. Ltd.
**IT Services & Product Engineering**
Saarthi (meaning "guide") is our flagship IT arm, delivering bespoke software development, digital transformation services, cloud solutions, and product engineering expertise. We partner with enterprises worldwide to architect, build, and scale robust technological solutions that drive efficiency and competitive advantage.

#### Agni Pharma Innovations (API)
**Pharmaceutical R&D & Biotechnology**
API is at the forefront of pharmaceutical research and development, focusing on novel drug discovery, clinical trials management, and the development of advanced biopharmaceutical solutions. We are committed to addressing unmet medical needs and improving global health outcomes through rigorous science and innovative therapies.

#### Vajra Solutions
**Advanced Analytics, AI/ML & Data Science**
Vajra (meaning "thunderbolt") harnesses the power of data, providing advanced analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence solutions. We transform complex datasets into actionable insights, enabling businesses to make smarter decisions, optimize operations, and unlock new growth opportunities across various industries.

#### Praxis Global Logistics Tech
**Supply Chain & Logistics Technology**
Praxis specializes in developing and implementing cutting-edge technology solutions for supply chain optimization and logistics management. From intelligent warehousing systems to predictive freight analytics, we ensure seamless, efficient, and resilient global movement of goods, critical especially for the pharmaceutical and manufacturing sectors.

#### Nectar Digital Marketing & Experience
**Digital Strategy, Branding & Customer Experience**
Nectar is our full-service digital agency, crafting compelling brand narratives, executing data-driven digital marketing campaigns, and designing immersive customer experiences. We help our clients, including Agni Holdings' own subsidiaries, connect with their audiences and achieve digital leadership.
